The cloud journey

They moved ~70% to the cloud. Then they hit the heart.

Over the last few years the bank ran an aggressive modernization program and migrated roughly 70% of its estate to AWS. By most measures, a huge win. But the remaining 30% isn't the leftover — it's the critical core: deposits, payments, lending, treasury. The monetary heart of the bank.

Deposits alone hold 40M+ accounts, and ~95% of them still live on a legacy AS/400 core built over three decades. You can't rip that out in a weekend. Touch it wrong and people can't see their money.
